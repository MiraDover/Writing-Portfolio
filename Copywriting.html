Cover girl for Gamer girls

This is a magazine ad layout for a fictitious campaign in which the well known makeup company COVERGIRL seeks to target female gamers. Poster copy for a cereal bar

This is a fictitious poster designed for a cereal bar company in a Media Arts and Design class. The idea behind the headline is to pull in the viewer with something familliar and playful. Then, the slogan and body copy keep that playful tone while encouraging the creativity of the viewer.

Created in Adobe Illustrator. September 2022.
